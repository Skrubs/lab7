About Rooks and Kings

Rooks and Kings known to most as RnK was innovative in their tactics. Leading the way for others to adopt their fighting style. First they came up with the Triage tactics that run wild in New Eden today, and then they shifted their toolkit to the relentless Battleship pipe bombing strategy where they would decimate extremely large fleets of 200+ foes with only 20 people. Today they remain undefeated.

What happened to Rooks and Kings

Through Reddit and other channels there are a lot of rumors of what happened to Rooks and Kings, and as a director and core member of Rooks and Kings I can see none of what you read is true. What happened to us is simple, it's the same thing that happens to most....Time. A lot of us are older now with families and more responsibilities. We are all still friends and great gaming community, but we just can't devote the time it requires to EVE Online anymore.
